1. Introduction

ALBUKHR Investment Limited is a community-driven investment platform designed to empower individuals and communities through real-world industries, digital innovation, and transparent governance.

The platform allows users to invest into industries such as agriculture, food production, transport, healthcare, and infrastructure development.

2. Vision

To build a global community-driven investment ecosystem that supports real industries and empowers communities worldwide.

3. Mission

To provide accessible investment opportunities, transparent governance, and sustainable economic growth through real-world industries.

4. Ecosystem Architecture

  • Treasury Layer
  • Governance Layer
  • Security Layer
  • Transparency Layer

5. Investment Model

Users stake Pi into projects and earn rewards based on project performance and lock duration.
