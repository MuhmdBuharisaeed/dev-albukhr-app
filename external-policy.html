ALBUKHR External Policy
External Project Guidelines
English:
All external projects submitted to ALBUKHR ecosystem must follow these guidelines to ensure transparency, security, and investor protection.

Hausa:
Dukkanin external projects da aka shigar cikin ALBUKHR ecosystem dole su bi waɗannan ƙa'idoji domin tabbatar da gaskiya, tsaro, da kariyar masu saka jari.
1. Project Ownership
English:
Project owner must provide accurate information about the project and funding purpose.

Hausa:
Mai project dole ya bayar da sahihin bayani game da project da manufar amfani da kuɗi.
2. Initial Liquidity Requirement
English:
External projects may require initial liquidity before becoming active.

Hausa:
External projects na iya buƙatar initial liquidity kafin su zama active.
3. Admin Approval Required
English:
All external projects must be reviewed and approved by ALBUKHR admin.

Hausa:
Dukkan external projects dole su wuce binciken admin kafin su bayyana.
4. Escrow Protection
English:
Funds may be escrow-locked for investor protection.

Hausa:
Ana iya kulle kuɗi cikin escrow domin kare masu saka jari.
5. Liquidity Management
English:
External project owners can add or withdraw liquidity through funder dashboard.

Hausa:
Masu external project zasu iya ƙara ko cire liquidity ta funder dashboard.
6. Risk Disclosure
English:
External investments carry risk. Investors must invest responsibly.

Hausa:
External investment na da haɗari. Masu saka jari su yi hankali.
7. Admin Control
English:
ALBUKHR reserves the right to freeze or review any external project.

Hausa:
ALBUKHR na da ikon dakatar ko bincikar kowanne external project.
8. Community Protection
English:
Projects violating policies may be removed.

Hausa:
Project da ya karya doka za a iya cire shi daga ecosystem.
